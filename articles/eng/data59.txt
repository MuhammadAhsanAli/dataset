police in sweden probe rape reportedly aired on facebook
file photo - the facebook logo is pictured at the facebook headquarters in menlo park, california.  (reuters) (reuters)three men have been arrested in sweden on suspicion of participating in a group rape that was streamed live on a closed facebook group, swedish authorities said tuesday.police spokeswoman lisa sannervik said the investigation into "a serious sexual offense" was in a preliminary phase and she could not provide further details. no charges have been pressed.sannervik said a teenager and two men in their 20s were arrested sunday in the city of uppsala, north of stockholm, after police received tips about the streaming and rape from users.
